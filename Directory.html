Joining Troop 659 >>>

    If you are a current and active member of St Marina Boy Scouts Troop 659, then your parents should have received an email inviting them to join www.scoutbook.com. Scoutbook.com is a powerful multi-platform software (internet and mobile device) that allows a parent and child to have updates and access to the scout’s merit badge, rank advancements, awards and any other detail related the scout’s involvement with Troop 659.


    If you have not yet received this email invitation, please reach out to your patrol leader, scoutmaster or committee member for help. Please Note: many generic email servers like: hotmail, AOL, yahoo and gmail, may send this email to Junk/Spam. So be sure to check there too.
