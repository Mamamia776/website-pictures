Joining Troop 659

 
 
 
 
 

    Every year troop 659 hopes to welcome in new scouts as they grow and reach the minimum threshold for admittance (6th grade or higher). As a new member you may need some guidance on what to do and how to do it. Relying on fellow scouts is a great resource - but the purpose of this website is to empower you with information 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.


    In the column to the right of this paragraph you will find a heading “IMPORTANT LINKS”. Here you will find the necessary forms and information you need to get started. Please remember: you are not an official scout of Troop 659 until you have passed the minimum requirements for joining troop 659. Our two prerequisites are; you must be in 6th grade or higher and you must be an active deacon in the Coptic Orthodox Church. The New Member Handout will also let you know the other things you will need to know, understand and have to be an active member.


Stay Updated

        This website has an “Updates” page that will contain all the updated information about what is going on in the Boy Scouts Troop 659. It is your job to check this on a regular basis. If you would like to be emailed when the website is updated, then be sure to provide us with your email address on your application.


Important Dates

          Troop 659 is open for new scouts starting from September to the end of October. New Enrollment Closes October 31 of every year. Troop 659 has to submit member information every year in November to renew our annual Boy Scout charter. Any scout who does not submit an application before October 31 will have to wait until the follow year.
